Practical advice you can use today

Slow down on urgency. If an email asks for passwords, payments, gift cards, account resets, or document review under pressure, verify it using a known phone number or a fresh message before you act.

Email security works best when technology and human caution work together. A quick pause is often the difference between a safe office and a preventable incident.
